Formed in the artic circle in 2012 by lead vocalist and guitar player Selia Burg and bassist Arf Dive, PSYCHEDELIC.SEALS quickly rose to the top of charts with their attention grabbing sound. Soon after their inception they added drummer Sealene North and backup vocalist José. They found a large audience around the north pole and soon after signed with Frozen Records. They have since gone on make 4 albums, as well as 2 singles.
